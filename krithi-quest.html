KRITHI QUEST (Technical Quiz)

Description

KRITHI QUEST is an exciting quiz exploring civil engineering concepts. Participants enjoy the thrill of problem-solving, team competition, and can win prizes or participation certificates. Questions cover the civil engineering domain.

Rules & Guidelines

  • A team consists of two members (B.Tech., Civil Engineering Students).
  • Preliminary test will be conducted to select SIX TEAMS for the final quiz.
  • Final quiz has 3 rounds: Team Question Round, Picture Question Round, Rapid Fire Round.
  • Three prizes for 1st, 2nd, and 3rd positions.
