Civil Digi-Expo (Digital Poster Presentation)

Description

Civil Digi-Expo promotes digital poster presentations on trends in civil engineering. Students present innovative ideas in PDF/image format.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Participation limited to Civil Engineering students.
  • Max two participants per poster.
  • Present Digital Poster in single PDF or image format.
  • Description to be presented during the session.
