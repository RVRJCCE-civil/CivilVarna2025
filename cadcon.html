CADCon (Technical Design Competition)

Description

CADCon challenges participants to showcase creativity, accuracy, and command over AutoCAD software. Participants apply drafting skills on real-world problems and receive an on-the-spot task to complete within 90 minutes.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Participation is limited to individual Civil Engineering student only.
  • Screening test may be conducted to shortlist participants.
  • Participants will draw plan for the given problem statement on the spot.
  • Use of mobile phones, internet, or external devices is not allowed.
