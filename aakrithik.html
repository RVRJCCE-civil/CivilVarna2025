üìù Description

Aakrithik tests participants' ability to translate theoretical concepts into a **stable, load-bearing physical structure**. Teams are challenged to design and construct a miniature **bridge model** using specific materials within a limited time. The model will be judged not only on its design and aesthetics but, most importantly, on its **efficiency** (Failure load to Dead weight).

üìú Rules and Guidelines

  • It is a **team event** with a minimum of **2 members** with an objective of fabricating a bridge model of your choice in a fixed time.
  • **Allowed Materials:** Sticks (unlimited), Gum, Cutter, and Thread only, which will be provided at the time of the event.
  • **Selection Criteria:** Based on Architecture, Dead weight, and **Maximum load** measured at **30 cm span**, **11 cm width**, and length not less than **40 cm**.
  • A maximum of **12 teams** will be shortlisted if more are registered, by a screening test comprising short answers/multiple-choice questions in Solid mechanics, Structural analysis, and other general topics.
  • Duration of model making: **1 hr 30 Min + 10 min Grace**.
  • There are three prizes to win (1st, 2nd, and 3rd).
