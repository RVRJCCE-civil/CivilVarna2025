AAKRITHIK (Model Making Contest)

Description

AAKRITHIK tests participants' ability to translate concepts into a stable, load-bearing bridge model. Teams design and construct miniature models within the given time.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Team event with minimum 2 members.
  • Materials allowed: sticks, gum, cutter, thread.
  • Models judged on architecture, dead weight, and max load.
  • Duration: 1hr 30min + 10min grace. Max 12 teams shortlisted if needed.
