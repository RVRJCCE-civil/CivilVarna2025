Civil Digi-Expo is a digital Poster presentation contest, focusing on the communication skills required for engineers to deliver technical knowledge effectively. Participants are encouraged to deliver a digital poster on one of the following themes:

  • Trends in Concrete Technology.
  • Green Building Technology.
  • Watershed management.
  • Application of Geosynthetics in Civil Engineering.
  • Intelligent Transport Systems.
  • Applications of GIS in Civil Engineering.
  • Low Cost Housing Technologies.
