SAMSODHANA (Technical Paper Presentation)

Description

SAMSODHANA encourages students to review literature, prepare technical reports, present them, and improve communication and presentation skills. Authors can submit papers with novel ideas in civil engineering.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Open to all civil engineering students with novel ideas.
  • Maximum two authors per paper.
  • Papers in MS Word, Times New Roman 12pt, max six A4 pages.
  • Submit via Google form by 18-12-2025. Selection info sent by 22-12-2025.
